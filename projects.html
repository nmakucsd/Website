Project

Every year, Triton XR recruits over 100 students to build innovative XR applications across project teams ranging from beginner to advanced skill levels. Any passionate member with a unique idea can start a project, while we provide equipment, mentorship, space, leadership, publicity, and key connections to help them get off the ground and turn that idea into (extended) reality.


Past Project

To see past projects, view their work, and learn more about their ideas, visit our GitHub organization at https://github.com/TritonXR. For more information about each project, select a repository and navigate to the “Wiki” tab at the top. Our recent teams will have several pages that you can navigate and view to discover more about the team’s efforts and progress.
